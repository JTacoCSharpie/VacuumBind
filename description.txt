VacBinds is a server-wide item vacuum mod

Tap a keybind or set it to one of the automatic 
modes and gather up any loose items in world with 
no laggy item vortexes or out-of-range items.

Features:
3 Modes - Manual, Auto, & Drop Count
* Manual: Press of a button
* Auto: Every X second interval
* Drop Count: Automatic, but only if > than X items
